# Product Overview

TechieNeighbor is a professional technology services business serving Gwinnett County and Metro Atlanta. The company provides custom-built tech solutions with a personal touch.

## Core Services

- **Custom Website Development**: Professional web development for local businesses, from startups to established enterprises
- **Smart Home Automation**: Custom smart home solutions and home improvement technology
- **Local SEO & Marketing**: Targeted local SEO and marketing strategies for Metro Atlanta businesses
- **Website Maintenance**: Security updates, performance optimization, and regular backups

## Target Audience

- Local businesses in Gwinnett County and Metro Atlanta
- Homeowners seeking smart home solutions
- Small to medium-sized enterprises needing custom web applications

## Brand Identity

- Emphasizes the "personal touch" and custom solutions over off-the-shelf products
- Positions as a trusted local expert ("your neighbor")
- Focus on care, expertise, and community connection
- Primary color: Teal (#14b8a6, #0d9488)

## Key Differentiators

- Local, personalized service
- Custom solutions tailored to specific needs
- Expertise in both web development and home automation
- Strong focus on Gwinnett County and Metro Atlanta market
